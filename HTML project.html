My Study Abroad Trip

This summer I will be spending a month in Greece and Italy with a program called LeadAbroad, studying language and culture. I will be living in an apartment with five other girls that I have never met. In addition to studying abroad, we will also participate in multiple service opportunities in the local community.

While I'm overseas, I will be assiting in efforts to stop human trafficking in Europe. We will be working alongside victims of human trafficking to promote awareness amongst the locals. In addition, we will also be repairing an orphanage in Santorini that has become damaged and run-down over the past few years. While women have always been the primary target of human trafficking, recently, children have become another preferred target.
